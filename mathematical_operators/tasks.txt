2.	Make a program that calculates and prints out the average speed. Ask from the user distance and the time that was used. With those values program calculates the average speed and prints it to the user.

BMI	Weight          Status
Below 18.5	        Underweight
18.5 – 24.9	        Normal or Healthy Weight
25.0 – 29.9	        Overweight
30.0 and Above	    Obese
